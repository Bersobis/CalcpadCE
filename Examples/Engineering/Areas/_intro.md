[CalcpadCE](https://calcpad-ce.org) ships a worksheet for every common 2D shape, each one giving the [area](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Area) and the perimeter (or arc length) from a few labelled inputs.

Pick the shape you need — [square](#square), [circle](#circle), [rectangle](#rectangle), [regular polygon](#polygon), [triangle](#triangle), [parabola](#parabola), [ellipse](#ellipse), or one of the [sector](#sector-angle) / [segment](#segment-angle) variants — and override the inputs in the Desktop app; the result updates instantly.
