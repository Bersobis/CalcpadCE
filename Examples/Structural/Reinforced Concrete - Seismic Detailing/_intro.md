[CalcpadCE](https://calcpad-ce.org) worksheets in this section build the seismic detailing checks of [reinforced concrete](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reinforced_concrete) primary members of medium ductility class (DCM) according to [Eurocode 8](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eurocode_8): the geometry of the critical regions, the minimum and maximum reinforcement ratios, the spacing of the transverse reinforcement and the confinement requirements at the plastic hinges.

The [beam detailing sheet](#beam-detailing-for-dcm) covers a rectangular beam with a slab flange and verifies the longitudinal ratio, the spacing of the closed stirrups inside the critical region of length $l_{cr}$ and the lap-splice requirements for the bottom and top bars.
The [column detailing sheet](#column-detailing-for-dcm) checks the dimensions of the cross-section, the longitudinal ratio, the confinement of the boundary regions for the maximum seismic axial load and the minimum mechanical volumetric ratio of the hoops.
The [shear wall detailing sheet](#shear-wall-detailing-for-dcm) addresses the confined boundary elements at the wall ends, the web horizontal and vertical reinforcement and the curvature ductility verification at the base of the wall.
